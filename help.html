Understanding X Trends

  • What are X Trends? X Trends are trending topics or hashtags that are currently generating a lot of interest on Twitter.
  • How are Trends Ranked? XTrendWatch considers several factors to determine trending topics on Twitter:
    • Tweet Volume: The number of times a hashtag is mentioned in tweets within a specific timeframe.
    • Engagement: Metrics like retweets and replies to gauge user participation.
    • Velocity: The speed of a hashtag's popularity growth.
    • Location: Filtering trends based on geolocation data or user-specified locations.
  • Why Use X Trends? Following X trends can help you stay up-to-date on current events and connect with others.

Using XTrendWatch

  • Finding Trends: By default, XTrendWatch displays the top trending topics in your selected location. Switch between "Worldwide" and "Trending" tabs or choose a specific country.
  • Exploring Hashtags: The "Top Hashtags" section shows most tweeted or longest-trending hashtags for different timeframes.
  • Going Further: Clicking on a trend will likely take you to a Twitter search for that hashtag.
